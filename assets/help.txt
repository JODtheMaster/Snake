Need help with Snake?

MOVEMENT
Done using W,A,S,D.
You cannot turn in the opposite direction to
the one you are moving in (ie: you cannot go down
if you're moving up)

PAUSING AND EXITING
These are done by clicking on the two buttons at the top
of the game, next to the score and highscore.

SETTINGS
Settings can be accessed by PAUSING,
and also on the home screen. It allows you to:
- Change your speed
- Change which map you are playing

HIGHSCORES
This displays your top 3 all time scores.
